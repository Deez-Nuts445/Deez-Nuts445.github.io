ReDirectR(RDR) is a web redirecting sevice which is completly free to use. No limits, open-sourced, your immagination.


To use RDR, just simply put the url you want people to go to to the link below:

https://deez-nuts445.github.io/redir.html?params={"link":"put link here","time":"put time here"}

Default waittime is 5 seconds.
